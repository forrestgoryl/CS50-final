Instructions for changing email address:

1.) Enter the new email address, click submit.

2.) Go to Contact and submit a contact request (it doesn't matter what data is entered into the contact form).

3.) Go to your new email and find the formsubmit email in your new email, then activate formsubmit.

4.) Afterwards, take the string of numbers and letters that formsubmit provides, paste the string in the new email and confirm email fields, and resubmit.
